What's an Open Innovation Tournament? ü§î

An Open Innovation Tournament is a dynamic event that brings together diverse minds to solve real-world challenges. Inspired by the framework developed by Profs. Karl Ulrich and Christian Terwiesch at UPenn, it's a systematic approach to generating and evaluating innovative ideas through friendly competition.

üé• Watch: Innovation Tournaments Explained

Key features of our tournament:

  • Diverse Participation: Entrepreneurs, intrapreneurs, policymakers, and makers collaborate.
  • Rapid Innovation: From brainstorming to prototyping and pitching in just two days.
  • Real Impact: Solutions address pressing challenges in various sectors.
  • Networking: Connect with potential partners, mentors, and investors.

The tournament unfolds in two exciting stages:

  1. Open Ideation Round: Teams form, brainstorm, and create rapid prototypes. Top ideas advance via peer voting.
  2. Finals: Selected teams refine their ideas and pitch to expert judges. The most promising solutions win awards and potential support for further development.

Join us in this exhilarating journey of innovation and impact. Be part of shaping Philadelphia's future!
