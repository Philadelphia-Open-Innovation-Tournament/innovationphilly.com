🚀 How to Win

Five moves that separated last year’s champs from the pack. Tap any card 👇

1. Idea Storm 🌩️ — Quantity before quality

Start with a “no‑idea‑left‑behind” blast. Ask your frontier model to:

  • Map every constraint (“must use existing city data”, “zero hardware”) and intentionally violate each to spark fresh angles.
  • Combine random domains (e.g. healthcare × fantasy sports) for unexpected mash‑ups.
  • Generate at least 100 bullet ideas in under 10 minutes—it’s a numbers game.

Only then slice mercilessly until 3 concepts survive the cull.

2. Synthetic User Feedback 🗣️ — AI as your focus group

Create 3–5 rich personas (age, job, frustrations, wild opinions). Prompt the model to role‑play each:

  • “As Alex, a 34‑year‑old nurse, what’s the first thing you’d check on this landing page?”
  • “List three deal‑breakers that would stop you signing up.”

You’ll surface objections, jargon confusion, and killer feature ideas without scheduling a single Zoom call.

3. Prototype Lightning‑Fast ⚡ — Ship a page in 5 minutes

Use Manus (Starter/Basic plan) or a barebones Replit site:

  1. Paste your hero headline & pain‑point bullets.
  2. Let the AI designer pick a palette and stock icons.
  3. Press ⬆︎ Publish. Shareable URL = instant social proof.

Landing page > slide deck. Real links feel real.

4. Stress‑Test 🛡️ — Debate your idea to death

Run an internal red‑team session:

  • “Argue why this will never hit 10 k users.”
  • “List regulations that could shut us down.”
  • “Pretend you’re our fiercest competitor—how do you out‑innovate us?”

Patch every hole, or pivot early and save face.

5. Nail the Narrative 🎤 — Pitch like a trailer

Structure:

  1. Cold open (Problem): 15‑second pain story.
  2. Aha moment (Solution): demo gif or mock‑up gif.
  3. Impact montage: numbers, testimonials, future roadmap.

Rehearse Q&A with ChatGPT as a grumpy board member until nothing rattles you.
