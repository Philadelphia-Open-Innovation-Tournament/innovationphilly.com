🚀 How to Win

Five proven moves from past champions. Open each tip to dive deeper.

1. Idea Storm 🌩️

Kick things off with volume. Use AI to map the challenge space and generate hundreds of raw concepts. Don’t judge—just capture.

  • Prompt frontier models for “20 crazy + 20 practical + 20 hybrid” ideas.
  • Look for patterns, mashups, or surprising overlaps.
2. Instant User Feedback 🗣️

Create detailed personas (age, profession, pain‑points) and let AI role‑play interviews. The objections and desires surfaced here will save hours of guess‑work.

  • Ask: “If you had this solution tomorrow, what would delight/frustrate you?”
  • Refine positioning before writing a single line of code.
3. Prototype Light‑Speed ⚡

Translate sketches into clickable Figma frames, generate hero copy, or spin up a Flask/Python proof‑of‑concept directly from prompts. Show, don’t tell.

  • Use image generation for product renders.
  • Leverage low‑code to stitch APIs fast.
4. Stress‑Test 🛡️

Before the judges do, fire every “yeah‑but” you can imagine: market size? edge‑cases? legal hurdles? Have AI debate you; plug each hole or pivot early.

5. Nail the Narrative 🎤

Winning pitches feel like great trailers: clear protagonist, sharp conflict, big reveal. Storyboard with AI, craft punchy headlines, rehearse Q&A.

Toolkit: ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini (paid tiers) + agentic runner like Manus to chain tasks.
