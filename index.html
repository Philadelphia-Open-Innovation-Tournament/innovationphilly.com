~/ai_advantage Winning with AI

Based on insights from previous tournaments, here’s how to leverage AI for an edge:

▶️ Watch this short guide for tips on how to win the tournament.

01 · Idea Generation at Scale

Use AI to rapidly generate diverse ideas across different domains:

  • Domain Mapping: Have AI create a comprehensive map of the problem space.
  • Constraint Exploitation: Generate ideas that leverage or bypass constraints.
  • Combinatorial Thinking: Merge concepts from different fields for novel solutions.
  • Quantity First: Spray & pray — hundreds of ideas, then filter for gold.
02 · Synthetic Customer Feedback

Simulate diverse customer perspectives:

  • Persona Embodiment, Objection Anticipation, Edge Case ID, and more.
03 · Rapid Prototyping

Leverage AI to create functional prototypes across modalities (UI, copy, code, service maps…).

04 · Structured Criticism

Ask AI to tear your idea apart before the judges do.

05 · Presentation Optimization

Craft a killer narrative, visuals, and Q&A prep using frontier models.

Recommended AI Tool‑Stack

Bring at least one frontier model (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, Mistral) on a paid tier. Pair it with an agentic system like Manus for workflow automation. Replit or Perplexity can augment coding and research.

$ Top teams combine multiple tools rather than relying on a single platform.
