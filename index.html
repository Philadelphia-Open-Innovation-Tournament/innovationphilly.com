🚀 How to Win

Five moves that separated last year’s champs from the pack. Tap to expand 👇

1. Idea Storm 🌩️ — Quantity before quality

Fire a “no‑idea‑left‑behind” prompt at your model:

  • Break, bend, or reverse every stated constraint.
  • Cross‑pollinate random industries to spark novelty.
  • Hit 100 raw bullets in 10 min, then shortlist three keepers.
2. Synthetic User Feedback 🗣️ — AI focus group

Ask the model to role‑play customers, partners, even regulators. Extract emotional quotes and hidden objections fast.

3. Prototype Lightning‑Fast ⚡ — Ship a page in 5 min

Use Manus (Starter/Basic) or Replit:

  1. Paste your headline + pain bullets.
  2. Auto‑theme, hit publish.
  3. Share link → instant proof.
4. Stress‑Test 🛡️ — Debate to improve

Run an AI red‑team session: “Why would only 50 people care?” “What law could kill us?” Patch or pivot.

5. Nail the Narrative 🎤 — Pitch like a trailer

Problem → aha moment → impact montage. Rehearse Q&A with ChatGPT acting as your grumpiest investor.
