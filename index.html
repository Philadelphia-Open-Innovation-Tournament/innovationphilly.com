Philadelphia Open Innovation Tournament

An annual AI-first hackathon connecting Philadelphia's top innovators with corporate sponsors to solve real-world challenges through competitive collaboration.

What is the Philadelphia Open Innovation Tournament?

The Philadelphia Open Innovation Tournament is an annual competitive event that brings together diverse teams of innovators, technologists, and problem-solvers to tackle real-world challenges using cutting-edge AI tools and methodologies. Inspired by the innovation tournament framework developed by Professors Karl Ulrich and Christian Terwiesch at the University of Pennsylvania, our event creates a structured environment where creativity meets competition.

Each year, we partner with corporate sponsors to define a specific challenge or problem domain. Over an intensive weekend, participants work in teams to develop innovative solutions, leveraging AI-first approaches and modern technology stacks. Our rigorous judging process evaluates submissions based on innovation, feasibility, impact, and execution quality.

How It Works

Tournament Structure

Each Philadelphia Open Innovation Tournament follows a proven format designed to maximize creativity, collaboration, and output quality:

Phase 1: Challenge Definition & Team Formation

Working with our sponsor partners, we define a specific challenge or problem domain that addresses real business needs. Participants register individually or as teams, and we facilitate team formation to ensure diverse skill sets and perspectives.

Phase 2: Intensive Development Weekend

Teams gather for an intensive weekend of ideation, development, and iteration. We provide:

  • Access to AI tools and development resources
  • Mentorship from industry experts
  • Structured checkpoints and guidance
  • Collaborative workspace and networking opportunities
Phase 3: Judging & Recognition

Teams present their solutions to a panel of judges comprising sponsor representatives, industry experts, and community leaders. Evaluation criteria include:

  • Innovation and creativity
  • Technical execution and feasibility
  • Potential impact and scalability
  • Alignment with challenge objectives

Winners receive prizes, recognition, and opportunities for continued engagement with sponsor organizations.

AI-First Approach

Our tournament emphasizes AI-first methodologies, encouraging participants to leverage large language models, machine learning tools, and modern AI frameworks to accelerate development and enhance solution quality. This approach reflects the evolving landscape of technology and prepares participants for the future of work.
